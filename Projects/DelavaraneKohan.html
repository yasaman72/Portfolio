Delavarane Kohan

February 2017

Delavaran-e Kohan is a game made in a two-day game jam. It’s a competitive game with ATB mechanic and RPG elements. I was inspired by Persian miniature for the game’s art style.

  • Game Engine: Unity
  • My Role: Visual Designer, Game Designer, Animator

I enjoy trying new positions and practice new skills in a safe environment like game jams. I usually push my boundaries and play the roles that I don't usually follow professionally. For this game, I put myself in the shoe of an artist and tried to find a fitting and interesting art style for our short game development experience. I think it turned out great!
