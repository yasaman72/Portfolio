Homeboard

January 2019

Homeboard is a dungeon crawler gamer that uses all the keys on the keyboard. Your keyboard is your map. You start the game from Enter key on the keyboard and leave your home to start your journey. Go around and check different rooms on your keyboard. We made the game for Global Game Jam 2019.

  • Game Engine: Unity
  • My Role: Game Designer, Programmer

It was one of my first intense experiences of developing a game in short time as a programmer. I implemented most of the systems like the main game loop, the combat and UIs.
