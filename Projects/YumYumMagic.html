Yum Yum Magic

April 2020

You have to feed the monster by using magic to make the correct meal. The Nordic Game Jam and Ludum Dare were at the same time and we had the idea to combine both jam themes together. This game created with the combination of Keep it alive and Everyday Magic theme in mind.

  • Game Engine: Unity
  • My Role: Visual Designer

As most other game jams, I wanted to try something new and weird in this jam as well. So I wore the hat of visual designer and made this weird looking game.
