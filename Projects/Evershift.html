Evershift

October 2019 - January 2020

a VHS style inspired game that changes its look constantly, based on the player's performance. To escape from these unfortunate events, the player needs to find the mysterious places and play the mini-games to finish the game.

  • Game Engine: Unity
  • My Role: Programmer

As one of the programmers of the project, I made the procedural generation system of the jungle, based on a predefined shape of the jungle area. I also implemented some of the mini games and the word puzzle.
