Evershift

October 2019 - January 2020

An endless arcade we made for 2017 Global Game Jam with "wave" theme. The player controls a gap between two lines by using their voice to avoid obstacles. The idea comes from the mantras used in zen, in combination with zen garden visuals.

  • Game Engine: Unity
  • My Role: Programmer

In our team of two, my partner and I designed the game. I wore the hat of the visual design to enjoy some visual development. I am enchanted by the idea of doing something new and trying a new skill in game jams.
