Divers Kingdom

June 2021

This game was made for GMTK game jam 2021 with the theme Joined Together. Divers Kingdom is a free-fall in an endless sky where you should grab hands and legs of others divers to make various formations.

  • Game Engine: Unity
  • My Role: Programmer

As one of the programmers, I made the grabbing system and animation, the scoring and progression system.
