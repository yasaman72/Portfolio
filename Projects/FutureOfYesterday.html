Future of Yesterday (VR)

December 2018

Explore an exemplary apartment full of new technology as it was imagined during a time that the most of us only know from old movies and classical music. In this single player, sandbox VR game it is up to you how and in which order you try out all the things that your environment provides you with.

  • Game Engine: Unreal
  • My Role: Programmer

This is my very first project in Unreal game engine. I was also new to programming and VR development. I mostly used blueprints in this project and implemented the basic VR controls and all interactable objects like the anti gravity and scale gun and a little cleaning robot!
