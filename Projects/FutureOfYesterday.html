Future of Yesterday

December 2018

Explore an exemplary apartment full of new technology as it was imagined during a time that the most of us only know from old movies and classical music. In this single player, sandbox style game it is up to you how and in which order you try out all the things that your environment provides you with. We have created this VR experience for all you young people and adults who are interested in possible future technology!

  • Game Engine: Unreal
  • My Role: Programmer

In our team of two, my partner and I designed the game. I wore the hat of the visual design to enjoy some visual development. I am enchanted by the idea of doing something new and trying a new skill in game jams.
