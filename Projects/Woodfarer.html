Woodfarer

December 2017 - March 2018

Woodfarer is a Forest-Crawling game in which you explore different areas of a cursed forest and gather spirits to lead them to the exit. The surrounding world is always new, and you need to map it out in order to explore it easier. You can use the spirits you've collected to fight off enemies and even solve some puzzles. There are many exploration options in the game, and items to increase your health and a chance to build up your score, incentive to see all there is to be found. Woodfarer is a Cologne Game Lab BA1 student collaborative project and still under development.
